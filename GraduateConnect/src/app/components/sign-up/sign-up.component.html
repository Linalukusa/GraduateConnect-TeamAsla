About Us

Graduate Connect is more than just a job portal, by analysing your academic skills and character traits we are able to bridge the gap between the academic and corporate IT worlds. We don't simply finding you positions which match your ICTS skills, but connect you with companies with cultures best suited for your personality type. Allowing you to grow in an environment best suited for you.

What makes us different?

We understand how daunting it can be entering the ICTS job market. A company's culture is so often a mystery until you actually start working there. Graduate Connect aims to remove the guess work from job searching by connecting graduates with potential ICTS employers. We understand the important role the environment and company culture plays on performance and employee satisfaction and through our culture fit assessment we are able to connect graduates with companies which they would want to work for.

Culture Fit

Graduate Connect makes use of a culture fit assessment to match graduates personality types with company cultures best suited for them. Based off Marston's Personality Theory, this culture fit assesment identifies where graduates fall across 8 different company cultures. Theres results are then used to match said graduates with companies having a similar culture.
